Delete Your Account

HMS ColregsCards by Help Me Sail

How to delete your account

  1. Open the HMS ColregsCards app
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top right of the home screen)
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm by tapping Yes, Delete

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile (name, email, avatar)
  • Your authentication credentials
  • Any poll votes associated with your account

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is not affected by account deletion. You can clear it by uninstalling the app:

  • Quiz scores and card mastery progress
  • Theme preferences

If you have an active subscription, it will not be automatically cancelled. Please cancel your subscription through your device's app store settings before deleting your account.

Can't access the app?

If you cannot access the app and need your account deleted, email us at bosun@helpmesail.com with the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.
