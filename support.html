Common Questions

  • How do I restore my subscription? Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store settings, not within the app.
  • How do I delete my account? Open the app, go to Settings, scroll to the Account section, and tap "Delete Account".
  • My progress was lost. Quiz progress is stored on your device. If you sign in, your progress syncs across devices.
